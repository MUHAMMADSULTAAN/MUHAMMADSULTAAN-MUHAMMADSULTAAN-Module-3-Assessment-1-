Ingredients

  • 1 cup Strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup Blueberries
  • 1 cup Kiwi, diced
  • 1 cup Pineapple, diced
  • 1 Banana, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons Honey
  • Juice of 1 Lime

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, pineapple, and banana.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together honey and lime juice.
  3. Pour the dressing over the fruit and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator before serving.
