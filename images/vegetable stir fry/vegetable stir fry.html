Ingredients

  • 2 cups Broccoli florets
  • 1 cup Bell Peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup Carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup Snap Peas
  • 1/2 cup Onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic, minced
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion, saut√©ing until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas to the skillet.
  4. Stir-fry the vegetables for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the vegetables and stir well.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
