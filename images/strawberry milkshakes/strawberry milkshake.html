Ingredients

  • 2 cups Fresh Strawberries, hulled
  • 1 cup Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar (optional)
  • Whipped Cream for topping
  • Fresh Strawberries for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine fresh strawberries, vanilla ice cream, milk, and sugar (if using).
  2. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the milkshake into glasses and top with whipped cream.
  4. Garnish with fresh strawberries before serving.
