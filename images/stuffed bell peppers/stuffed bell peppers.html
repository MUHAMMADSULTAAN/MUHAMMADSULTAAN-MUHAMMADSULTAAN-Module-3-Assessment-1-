Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients

  • 4 Bell Peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup Cooked Rice
  • 1 cup Black Beans, rinsed
  • 1 cup Corn
  • 1 cup Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Chili Powder
  • 1 cup Shredded Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together cooked rice, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff the mixture into each bell pepper and place them upright in a baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top of each pepper.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
