Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts, diced
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1-inch Ginger, grated
  • 1 can Coconut Milk
  • 2 tablespoons Curry Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Chopped Cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger; cook for another minute.
  3. Add diced chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in curry powder, salt, and pepper; cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped cilantro.
